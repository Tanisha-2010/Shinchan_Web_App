Shinchan Nohara

A Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Yoshito Usui

Hii!


Family

Shinchan is a 5 year old naughty toddler also known as Shinosuke Nohara. He lives in Kasukabe, Japan with his mom Mitsi Nohara also known as Misae Nohara, his dad Harry Nohara also known as Hiroshi Nohara, his little sister Himawari and his pet dog Shiro.


FRIENDS & School

Shinchan goes to Futaba Kindergarden School. His close friends are Masao Sato, Bo Suzuki, Nene Sakurada, Toru Kazama, Ai-Chan Suotome. They are taught by their three teachers. Midori Yoshinaga Ishizaka is the 24 year old class teacher of the Sunflower class. Ume Matsuzaka is the 24 year old class teacher of the Rose Class. Masumi Ageo is the 23 year old class teacher of the Tulip Class. The principal of their school is Bunta Takakuro.
